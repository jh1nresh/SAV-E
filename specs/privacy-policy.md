# SAV-E Privacy Policy

_Draft — review before publishing. Host at `https://sav-e.app/privacy` (required
by App Store Connect). Last updated: 2026-06-25._

SAV-E is a private place-memory app. This policy explains what we collect, why,
and the choices you have. SAV-E is built to be private by default: your saved
places are yours, and there is no public feed.

## What we collect

**Account.** When you sign in (Apple, Google, or email via our auth provider
Privy), we receive an account identifier and, depending on the method, your
email address. We use this only to create and secure your account.

**Your saved content.** Places you save, review candidates, notes, the source
links you add, and any reviews you write are stored in our backend so your map
syncs across your devices.

**Location.** With your permission, we use your device's location to show nearby
places and answer "what's near me" style questions. Location is used to serve
results in the moment; we do not sell it or use it to build an advertising
profile.

**Search text.** When you ask SAV-E a question or paste a link, that text is
sent to our place and AI providers (Google Places, Google Gemini) to identify
the place or answer your query.

We do **not** collect contacts, browsing history, or advertising identifiers,
and SAV-E has no ads.

## How we use it

- To provide the core features: saving, finding, confirming, and searching your places.
- To sync your private map across your devices.
- To keep your account secure.

We do **not** sell your personal data, and we do not show your saved places to
other users or post them publicly. Sharing only happens when you explicitly
create a shareable link.

## Third-party services

| Provider | Purpose | Data shared |
|---|---|---|
| Privy | Sign-in / account | Account identifier, email |
| Supabase | Encrypted backend storage | Your saved content, profile |
| Google Places | Resolve and enrich places | Search text, place queries |
| Google Gemini | Answer questions about your places | Query text |
| Apple / Google Maps | Open directions | Place coordinates (on tap) |

Each provider processes data under its own terms.

## Data retention & deletion

Your saved content stays until you delete it. You can delete individual places
in the app. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the
support address below; we will remove it within 30 days.

## Children

SAV-E is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their
data.

## Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be noted here with a new date.
